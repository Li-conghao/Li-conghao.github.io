Selected Projects

Edge‑Computing‑Enabled Multi‑Orbit Satellite Monitoring

National Undergraduate Innovation Project — Sep 2025 – Present

Led a national innovation project titled Xingdian Xunlian on a collaborative monitoring system leveraging edge computing and hybrid GEO/LEO communications. Our team designed a system for remote power‑line inspection and environmental monitoring, integrating edge AI to enable on‑site intelligence. I coordinated system design, technical roadmap and deliverables with advisors and industry partners.

Graph‑Informed Dynamic Inter‑Layer Link Allocation

Co‑author — 2026

Co‑authored a manuscript for IEEE GLOBECOM 2026 exploring graph‑informed strategies for dynamic inter‑layer link allocation in multi‑layer LEO satellite networks. Developed algorithms to schedule inter‑layer links based on network topology and traffic demand, improving connectivity and robustness.

QoS‑Aware Downlink Paging Control

First author — 2026

Designed downlink paging control schemes for UAV‑assisted 5G‑Advanced networks with on‑demand coverage. Implemented simulations to evaluate delay, reliability and resource utilisation, culminating in a publication in Drones.

Multi‑Feature Fusion for Dynamic User Clustering

Contributor — 2025

Contributed to the development of a multi‑feature fusion approach for dynamic user clustering in LEO satellite group handover scenarios. Responsible for simulation and result analysis for the 5th International Conference on Frontiers of Electronics, Information and Computation Technologies (ICFEICT 2025).
